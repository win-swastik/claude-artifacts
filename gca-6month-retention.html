Methodology: For each month M, we identify all unique clients who completed a withdrawal in months M-6 through M-1 (the "prior 6-month cohort"). We then check how many of those same clients also completed a withdrawal in month M. Retention % = retained / prior 6m cohort × 100. Data source: withdrawal table, status = 'completed', from [PROD] GCA Read Replica.
